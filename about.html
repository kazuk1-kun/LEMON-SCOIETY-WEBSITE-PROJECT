Welcome to Lemon Society!

Lemon Society is a non-governmental organization based in Assam, India, dedicated to the promotion, protection, and sustainable development of Assam Lemon and other lemon varieties grown in the region.

The Society works closely with farmers, researchers, local communities, and institutions to improve cultivation practices, enhance market access, and create awareness about the nutritional, economic, and cultural importance of lemons from Assam.

Through training programs, awareness campaigns, collaborative projects, and documentation of traditional knowledge, Lemon Society aims to build a strong and inclusive ecosystem around lemon cultivation and related livelihood opportunities.

AIM

To promote sustainable cultivation, value addition, and recognition of Assam Lemon and other regional lemon varieties, while improving the socio-economic conditions of farmers and communities associated with lemon-based livelihoods.

OBJECTIVES

  • To support farmers with training on improved and sustainable lemon cultivation practices.
  • To facilitate connections between farmers, markets, researchers, and government bodies.
  • To encourage value-added products from Assam Lemon, such as pickles, beverages, and essential oils.
  • To document and preserve traditional knowledge related to lemon cultivation and usage in Assam.
  • To create awareness about the nutritional and health benefits of lemons among the public.
  • To promote Assam Lemon as a unique regional identity and potential GI (Geographical Indication) product.

COMMITTEE MEMBERS

The Lemon Society is guided by a core committee consisting of experienced individuals from agricultural, academic, and community backgrounds.
